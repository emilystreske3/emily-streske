Governance & Compliance

Cybersecurity Compliance Framework Implementation

Designed and operationalized a compliance framework aligned to NIST CSF and SOC 2 Type II requirements. Defined control ownership, evidence collection routines, and a repeatable audit preparation process adopted across three business units.

  • Standardized evidence collection workflows, reducing audit preparation time
  • Established control owner accountability model with quarterly review cadence
  • Aligned security policies to regulatory requirements across multiple frameworks

Vendor Risk Management

Third-Party Risk Program Design

Built a vendor risk intake and monitoring program connecting procurement, security, and legal stakeholders. Created tiered risk criteria, intake questionnaire, and escalation process to surface high-risk vendor decisions before contract execution.

  • Introduced structured vendor risk tiers based on data access and service criticality
  • Reduced contract review cycle time by clarifying security requirements up front
  • Improved executive visibility through a monthly vendor risk summary report

Incident Response

Incident Response Playbook Development

Developed an incident response playbook covering detection, escalation, containment, communications, and post-incident review. Conducted tabletop exercises with cross-functional leadership to test and refine the playbook before a real event occurs.

  • Documented clear escalation paths for security, legal, and executive stakeholders
  • Delivered two tabletop exercises identifying gaps in communications and recovery
  • Established post-incident review process to capture lessons learned

Operational Leadership

Regulatory Readiness & Audit Preparation

Led a regulatory readiness initiative preparing the organization for an external compliance assessment. Coordinated evidence gathering across IT, HR, legal, and finance teams and managed remediation of identified control gaps through to closure.

  • Completed assessment preparation on schedule with no critical finding surprises
  • Closed 14 identified control gaps through structured remediation tracking
  • Created a reusable evidence library reducing future audit preparation effort
